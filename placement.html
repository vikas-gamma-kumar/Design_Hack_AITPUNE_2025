Vision and Mission

Vision


  • To enhance the employability of AIT students by imparting necessary knowledge and skills through the best training initiatives to meet the expectations of all types of Industries, with necessary interaction with the industry and alumni.

Mission


  • To develop flexible training module from first year to last year to cater to the need of a bright engineering future.

  • To achieve 100% placement of students in IT Product, Consulting, Fintech, Start-ups and other emerging domains by the year 2024.

  • To coordinate effective and systematic soft skills, personality development and industry specific training for all AIT students, so that they pass out with high employability skills.

  • To promote meaningful alumni interaction so that they become effective lifelong partners for achievement of AIT`s vision.

  • To achieve good branding of AIT amongst industry and army fraternity by organizing institute - industry interactions and showcasing the top ranking in all national surveys.

  • To develop training and placement cell as a career counseling center.

  • To act as an interface between Industry and Institute about their expectations of specific skill sets, value addition programs and emerging job profiles.
