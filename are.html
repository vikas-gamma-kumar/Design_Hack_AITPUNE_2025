`Career Building Through Core Engineering`


It is with great pride, we have launched our new program, "Automation and Robotics" at the Army Institute of Technology. This cutting-edge program is designed to equip our students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the rapidly evolving fields of automation and robotics.


In line with our commitment to providing students with a holistic education, this program has focus on experiential learning, innovation, and industry collaboration. Our state-of-the-art facilities and esteemed faculty members are eager to provide a conducive environment to explore and learn with vigor.


We are poised to produce leaders in the automation and robotics industry, driving innovation and development. This program is a positive step towards achieving our goal of nurturing talented engineers who make a meaningful impact on human life.
